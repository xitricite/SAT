State Administrative Tribunal

Level 6,
State Administrative Tribunal Building,
565 Hay Street,
PERTH WA 6000 or GPO Box U1991,
Perth 6845 (Next to the Perth City Library)

Office Hours
  • Counter inquiries: Sixth floor 8:30am to 4:30pm.
  • Telephone inquiries: 8:30am to 4:30pm.
  • Email: sat@justice.wa.gov.au
    (for sending information or submissions regarding matters currently before the Tribunal)
  • Phone: (08) 9219 3111 or 1300 306 017 (cost of a local call)
  • Fax: (08) 9325 5099
Parking and Access

Limited disabled parking bays on the SAT site may be booked by telephoning the SAT on (08) 9219 3111.
Drop-off bays are located right out the front of our building (565 Hay Street) on Hay Street, they cannot be used for parking.
